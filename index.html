Venue hire questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be a member?

A Hannans Club membership is required to host an event.

How do I secure a booking?

The venue fee and completed booking form are required at the time of booking.

When is final payment due?

Final payment and final numbers are due one week before the event.

Can I bring my own alcohol?

BYO beverages are not allowed because the club is fully licensed.

Do large events need security?

Events over 100 people require security arranged by the host.

What time do evening events finish?

Evening events conclude by midnight, with last drinks at 11.15pm.

Can we use outside catering?

Chef Raj from Raj's Copper Bowl operates independently from the Hannans Club kitchen. Hosts can contact Raj for a quote at rajscopperbowl@outlook.com or 0421 665 098.

Outside catering is welcome, with the host responsible for plates, cutlery, napkins, cleanup, and use of the bins provided.

Important booking notes

  • A Hannans Club membership is required to host an event.
  • The venue fee and completed booking form are required at booking.
  • Final payment and final numbers are due one week before the event.
  • Events over 100 people require security arranged by the host.
  • Evening events conclude by midnight, with last drinks at 11.15pm.
  • BYO beverages are not allowed because the club is fully licensed.

    Terms and conditions

    These are general venue hire conditions for Australian events and should be reviewed by The Hannans Club before public use.

    1. The Hirer is responsible for the conduct of guests, invitees, suppliers, contractors, and any person attending the event.
    2. Bookings are not confirmed until accepted by The Hannans Club and any required venue hire fee, deposit, or booking form has been received.
    3. Final numbers, event timings, room setup, catering arrangements, and supplier details must be confirmed by the date requested by The Hannans Club.
    4. All events must comply with the Liquor Control Act 1988 (WA), responsible service of alcohol obligations, venue licence conditions, and lawful directions from club staff.
    5. BYO alcohol is not permitted unless expressly approved in writing by The Hannans Club.
    6. The Hirer is responsible for damage, breakage, excessive cleaning, missing items, or costs caused by guests, suppliers, or contractors.
    7. External suppliers must comply with venue access, safety, insurance, cleanup, and pack-down requirements.
    8. The Hannans Club may require security, additional staffing, event management, or other controls for events based on guest numbers, event type, or risk.
    9. Decorations, candles, confetti, smoke machines, amplified sound, structures, and electrical equipment require prior approval.
    10. Noise, music, and entertainment must comply with venue requirements, licence conditions, and any local laws or directions.
    11. The Hirer must ensure minors are supervised and that no guest engages in unlawful, unsafe, offensive, or disorderly behaviour.
    12. The Hannans Club may refuse service, remove guests, end an event, or cancel a booking where safety, licensing, property, or conduct concerns arise.
    13. Cancellation, postponement, refund, and transfer requests are subject to The Hannans Club approval and any costs already incurred.
    14. The Hirer is responsible for obtaining any permits, approvals, licences, or insurances required for their event or suppliers.
    15. The Hannans Club is not liable for personal items, supplier property, or equipment left at the venue.
